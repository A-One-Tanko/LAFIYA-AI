# LAFIYA AI

### AI-Powered Healthcare for Hausa Speakers

**Health-to-Earn with GoodDollar**

LAFIYA AI is an AI-powered health assistant built specifically for Hausa-speaking communities across Nigeria and Africa. It delivers trusted, easy-to-understand health information in the Hausa language while rewarding users for learning about their health through GoodDollar.

By combining artificial intelligence, localized healthcare education, and digital financial inclusion, LAFIYA AI empowers people to make informed health decisions, earn GoodDollar (G$) rewards, and use those rewards to purchase airtime, mobile data, and pay utility bills.

---

## Why LAFIYA AI?

Millions of Hausa speakers face language barriers when accessing reliable digital health information. Most health resources are available only in English, leaving many people without access to information they can easily understand.

LAFIYA AI bridges this gap by providing culturally relevant health education in clear, natural Hausa, helping users make informed decisions about their health while promoting healthier communities.

## GoodDollar Integration

LAFIYA AI introduces a Health-to-Earn model powered by GoodDollar, combining health education with financial inclusion.

Users can:

- Read trusted health information in Hausa.
- Complete health awareness and learning activities.
- Earn daily GoodDollar (G$) rewards.
- Claim daily GoodDollar rewards.
- Use G$ to purchase mobile airtime.
- Use G$ to buy mobile data bundles.
- Pay utility bills using G$.
- Access financial tools powered by the GoodDollar ecosystem.
- Continue learning while earning digital rewards.
- Access emergency SOS features free of charge.

By rewarding healthy learning habits, LAFIYA AI makes healthcare education both engaging and economically beneficial for underserved communities.

## Vision

Our vision is to build Africa's leading AI-powered healthcare platform in indigenous languages, beginning with Hausa and expanding to other African languages.

We believe language should never be a barrier to quality healthcare or financial opportunity. By combining artificial intelligence with digital financial inclusion, LAFIYA AI empowers people to learn, stay healthy, earn rewards, and participate in the digital economy.
